"The Rising Up"

Kurt Latrell Alcantara founded Drippy Co. in 2021, driven by a shared passion for Basketball Culture and Streetwear Culture. What began as a simple dream evolved into a tangible product, born from the fusion of these two vibrant cultures.

Originating from 840 V. Javier St. Lagundi Morong Rizal, our journey mirrors a humble beginning, with operations centered around our home. As I traveled to various cities showcasing our designs, whether through wearing the shirts or advertising them, our community grew. Additionally, leveraging platforms like TikTok has significantly expanded our reach, resulting in an influx of orders.

Drippy Co. represents the underdogs, embodying the spirit of perseverance and determination. We stand for those who never made it and those striving to achieve their dreams. We celebrate the driven individuals—the ones who are first to arrive and last to leave—the embodiment of why the journey began.

Kurt Latrell Alcantara
